# Visual Quality V1 Art Bible

## Target
Upgrade the first BattleTest map into an original Joseon murim SRPG battlefield: Baekdu mountain sect gate, snow, ruins, torches, cliffs, danger terrain, and readable tactical lanes. Preserve all existing battle rules and tactical data.

## Palette
- Snow/night: cold blue gray, ink black, muted slate.
- Torch/accent: warm orange, dim gold, ember red.
- Allied accents: jade blue, pale cyan, restrained white.
- Enemy/danger: dark crimson, burnt orange, ash black.
- UI: dark ink panels, snow-light panels, jade and gold accents.

## Asset Folders
- `Art/VisualUpgradeV1/Concepts`

## Import Rules
- Tiles: Sprite, Single, Full Rect, PPU 64, high quality compression.
- Props/characters: Sprite, Single, Tight when safe, consistent PPU with character pipeline.
- VFX sheets: Sprite, Multiple, 4 frames left to right where applicable.
- UI: Sprite (2D and UI), no baked text, 9-slice when borders are authored.
- All generated images must avoid logos, watermarks, and readable text unless explicitly required.

## Battle Screen Goals
- At least 3 terrain material reads: snow, packed road/stairs, ice/stream/cliff.
- At least 2 warm torch sources contrasting cold ambient.
- Broken gate, torn banners, cover rocks, LOS blockers, hazards, and interactable props.
- Characters should feel embedded in the scene: shared tint, contact shadow, cast shadow, rim restraint, foot occlusion.
- Highlights must remain readable over painterly terrain.

## Originality
Do not copy existing commercial games, named UI systems, characters, icons, or logos. Keep every generated image original and adult/non-sexual for characters.
